About Us

10+ years of manufacturing excellence, trusted by global brands.

Who We Are

TONGYUAN TECH (Shenzhen Ruilian Technology Co., Ltd.) is a professional USB cable manufacturer established in 2013. Located in Shenzhen, China, we specialize in OEM/ODM production of USB-C, Lightning, Micro USB cables and accessories.

With a 10,000 sqm modern factory, 300+ skilled workers, and advanced production lines, we deliver 50M+ cables annually to 30+ countries. Our commitment to quality and innovation makes us the preferred partner for leading global brands.

Our Journey

Milestones

2013
Company Founded
Started as a small workshop in Shenzhen with 20 workers, focusing on Micro USB cables.
2015
MFi Certification
Obtained Apple MFi certification. Expanded to Lightning cable production for global clients.
2017
New Factory
Moved to 10,000 sqm modern facility with automated production lines. Monthly capacity reached 5M units.
2019
USB-C Expansion
Launched full USB-C product line including 100W PD cables and multiport hubs. Served 300+ clients.
2022
Global Reach
Expanded to 30+ countries. Established logistics partnerships for fast delivery worldwide. 500+ clients served.
2024
Innovation Continues
Launched 240W USB-C 2.1 cables and Qi2 wireless chargers. Annual output exceeds 50M units.
